## ❓ Cost FAQ — Questions Everyone Asks

**"Why do I pay Vapi and Twilio directly?"**
→ Because they are third-party services (like paying your phone bill to your carrier). I configure everything, but consumption is yours. You have total control. I do not touch a penny of that money.

**"What if I have no calls one month?"**
→ You only pay the Twilio number (~$1-3). Vapi only bills for minutes used. No surprises. No hidden fixed fees.

**"Can I stop anytime?"**
→ Yes. Stop refilling Vapi/Twilio. No cancellation fees. No penalties. You keep the configuration and can resume anytime.

**"Do you take a percentage on my calls?"**
→ No. I touch nothing on Vapi/Twilio. My revenue is the $2,500 setup only. You are fully autonomous after delivery.

**"What if prices go up?"**
→ Vapi and Twilio are established companies. Their prices have been stable for 3 years. If they change, you decide if you continue or not. You are not tied to a contract with me.

**"Who manages the Vapi/Twilio accounts?"**
→ I create them for you with your email (or a dedicated email). You are the legal owner. You have the credentials and total control. I keep nothing.

**"Are there any hidden fees?"**
→ No. This document is your guarantee. You see exactly what you pay and to whom. No line is hidden. No commission is taken.
