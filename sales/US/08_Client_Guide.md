# 9. FAQ — Daily Questions

**Q : Can the AI make a mistake on an appointment ?**
A : Yes, rarely (less than 10% of cases). Check your Google Calendar every morning for 2 minutes. The AI always confirms the date and time before hanging up, but a human check eliminates the remaining risk.

**Q : What if the patient speaks too fast or with a heavy accent ?**
A : The AI politely asks to repeat. If it truly does not understand, it offers to take a message or transfer to a human. You can listen to the transcript in the dashboard.

**Q : Can I listen to the calls ?**
A : Yes, if you enable recording in Vapi (paid option ~$0.02/min). Recordings appear in your dashboard. **Tip :** Enable recording during the first 30 days to verify quality.

**Q : Does the AI speak Spanish / French / Arabic ?**
A : Yes, per initial configuration. The AI automatically detects the caller's language and responds in the same language. If you want to add a language after delivery, contact me ($150).

**Q : What happens when I am on PTO ?**
A : The AI keeps answering. It books appointments and sends them to your calendar. You will see them when you return. You can block your PTO in Google Calendar so the AI does not book during your absence.

**Q : Can I have multiple AI numbers ?**
A : Yes, but each additional number requires a new setup ($2,500). Contact me for a custom quote.

**Q : What if I want to stop the service ?**
A : Stop refilling Vapi and Twilio. Your number will be deactivated after credit runs out. You keep the configuration and can resume anytime. **No cancellation fees.**

**Q : Does my human receptionist stay ?**
A : **Absolutely.** The AI is an assistant for your receptionist. She handles repetitive calls and off-hours. Your receptionist focuses on high-value tasks : in-person greeting, file management, premium patient relations. **It is win-win.**
